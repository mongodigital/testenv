Services

Working with Mongo, step by step.

Developing a Strategy
+

No one-size-fits-all approach here. Mongo designs a strategy that fits your vision and connects you with your ideal audience online — across the right channels, at the right time.

  • Define your advertising goals
  • Map how customers interact with your brand online
  • Build your ideal customer profile
  • Evaluate platforms, costs, and targeting options
Analytics Implementation
+

Once goals are established, we ensure every relevant action is being captured. A one-time setup that creates the data baseline your entire strategy will be built on.

  • Gauge marketing effectiveness and traffic quality
  • Optimize web design and user flow
  • Remove the guesswork — answer the 5 Ws of user data
Campaign Build Out
+

We've helped clients of varying goals, budgets, and experience levels excel online. Once strategy is set, we build your campaigns across chosen platforms — tailored to your exact needs.

  • Multi-platform deployment
  • Tailored to your goals and budget
  • Generally 2–5 days to build and test
Attribution & Insights
+

We don't set it and forget it. We continuously optimize and surface insights that get you the most for your ad spend — it's why 90%+ of our clients return for additional projects.

  • Traffic sources, volume, and time on site
  • Conversions and completed goals
  • Demographics: location, age, gender, interests
Consultation & Training
+

Want a trusted expert on call? Or to bring everything fully in-house? We offer complete training in Google Analytics and all relevant ad platforms, so your team can own the work with confidence.

  • Account-specific ongoing consultation
  • Complete in-house training programs
  • Google Analytics & platform deep-dives
